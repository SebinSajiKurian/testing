About Us

POST GRADUATE AND RESEARCH DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE

About the Department

The inception of Commerce Department is in 1977 with the pre-degree course. In 1982 the under graduate course B. Com was introduced. Now the department is offering B. Com. (Finance and Taxation) which is the most demanding one among the Commerce enthusiasts. The department was upgraded to Post Graduate Department in 1995 with the introduction of M. Com. (Finance) programme. Currently our department is a Research Centre which offers Ph.D. programme under the Faculty of Commerce, Mahatma Gandhi University ,Kottayam.
